## General info

WaWa Tabor is a small app that is desiganted to provide online location of all buses and trams in Warsaw each 10 - 15 seconds. The current positions of these means of transport are downloaded from the website of Miasto Sto≈Çeczne Warszawy (City of Warsaw) - https://api.um.warszawa.pl/. It was created only for academic purposes and it is not for commercial use. If you want to test this app, please go to Google Play Store and download it on your phone.
